I look up to historical figures who led their lives with principles and idealism.
Some of them are

  • Franklin D Roosevelt - He served as the 32nd President of the United States. He pulled the country out of the Great Depression and led the country to victory in WWII.
  • Shubhash Chandra Bose - He needs no introduction. He created Azad Hind Fauj and instilled fire of independence in millions of Indians.
  • Nelson Mandela - Inspired by the ideas of Mahatama Gandhi, He was instrumental in ending apartheid in South Africa. He spent 27 years in jail and also served as President of the country.
